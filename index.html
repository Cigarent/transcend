Introducing Transcend Madison, a cross-collaborative competition showcasing student innovations.

Our goal is to bring students from all across UW Madison to demonstrate their novel ideas and creations. Unlike previous competitions, anyone can compete—all you need is an idea.

However, this competition will combine all aspects of entrepreneurialism, so a winning idea exhibits:

  • a novel idea
  • a valid prototype
  • a tested business model
  • and most importantly, great execution

Sponsorship

In the past, competitions have fallen due to an unreliable or often single funding source—something we aim to fix! Transcend Engineering is an already established student organization which will act as a steady home for this competition. By finding awesome sponsors like yourselves, we will grow the Madison entrepreneurial together with our students in mind (which is Transcends sole mission as an org!).
